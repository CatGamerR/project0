Who is Luke Cage?
Luke Cage is a superhero with unbreakable skin that does hero jobs for a price. Essentially a mercenary.
What stories are collected in this volume?
Issues 1-16 of his original series are collected.
What are some of the high points of this volume?
If you like Blaxploitation films, you'll like this. It's also a collection of stories featuring an oft-overlooked hero. The idea of a superhero for hire is used well within the pages of these comics, with a few characters pointing out the selfishness inherent to mercenary work, and the character himself is a thrill.
What are some of the low points?
The title character himself is a stereotypical Angry Black Man, and one character in particular is an exaggerated caricature of a big black woman with thick red lips and a southern accent. Generally, the flaws inherent in the Blaxploitation genre are present in this blaxploitation comic.
Would I recommend this book?
Despite the dated stereotypes presented by the hero, I'd still say this book is worth a read for the premise. If you can enjoy it by looking past the stereotypes, it's a fine read. Give it a shot.
