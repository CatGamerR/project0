Taking Aim: Who is Green Arrow?

Green Arrow is a societal playboy by the name of Oliver Queen, whom I can best describe as a lighthearted version of Batman.

What stories are collected in this volume?

More Fun Comics 73-107, Adventure Comics 103-107, World's Finest Comics 7-28

What are the selling points of this book?

Seeing the origins of an obscure hero, plus a good laugh at the unoriginality sometimes. Also, the art is nice and colorful, which is a plus.

What are some of the weak points of this book?

There is a VERY dated stereotype of a Native American in one issue.

Would you recommend this book?

You're not missing out on much if you don't read this.
