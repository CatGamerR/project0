Fever Dreams: Who is the Sandman?

The Sandman is a crimefighter modeled in the vein of Batman: A wealthy playboy by the name of Wesley Dodds who fights crime in an intimidating manner. Later down the line, Dodds would be reinvented as a more traditional superhero in spandex with his own kid sidekick.

What stories are collected in this volume?

The entire Simon/Kirby run of the Sandman, as well as the one-shot 70s issue is collected here.

What are the selling points of this book?

Simon and Kirby's imagination and art are the highlights of this book. The art is eccentric and pops with dynamic action, and the plots carry that same eccentricity and ingenuity, with highlights like a villainous take on the Old Testament's Noah, the story of a boy who wants some long pants and gets entangled with a child-size mobster lookalike because of it, and the love story of an up=and-coming boxer trying to win both the title belt and the heart of the commentator.
